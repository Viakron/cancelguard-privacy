# cancelguard-privacy
Privacy Policy for CancelGuard
1. Information We Collect
CancelGuard is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly.
We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.
All information you enter into the app, including:
•	Subscription names
•	Renewal dates
•	Notes
•	Person labels
•	Subscription links
is stored locally on your device only.
 
2. How Your Information Is Used
Your data is used solely to provide the app’s functionality, including:
•	Displaying your subscriptions
•	Sending renewal reminders
•	Allowing you to manage your subscriptions
We do not access or process your data outside of your device.
 
3. Notifications
CancelGuard may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals.
These notifications are:
•	Generated on your device
•	Not sent from any external server
You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
 
4. Data Sharing
We do not:
•	Sell your data
•	Share your data
•	Transmit your data to third parties
CancelGuard does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
 
5. Internet Links
If you choose to add a subscription management link, tapping it will open an external website in your browser.
We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites.
 
6. Data Security
Because your data is stored locally on your device:
•	You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device
•	We do not have access to your data
 
7. Children’s Privacy
CancelGuard is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
 
8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
 
9. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
Email: support@viakron.com
Website: https://fitzstudio.app
 
Summary
CancelGuard keeps your data private by design:
•	Your data stays on your device
•	No tracking
•	No data collection
•	No sharing
Your information is yours.
